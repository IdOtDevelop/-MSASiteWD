Our Units


BASIC SCIENCE UNIT


Namma medicine(NM) has taken up the mission to strengthen the foundation of the future healers of the world. To do so, we conduct pre and para clinic classes for 1st and 2nd year medical students instructed by professional doctors. NM also lends supporting hand to NEET aspirants by guiding them along the process by conducting webinars and online classes.





ADVANCED CLINICAL STUDIES UNIT


As practical knowledge is the key to a good practitioner , NM provides platforms to enhance oneâ€™s medical knowledge and skills by conducting workshops and case based discussions with proficient doctors.





PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT UNIT


Since a Good Doctor is not just a healer by brain but a healer by heart too. Namma medicine focuses on the foundation and pillars of professionalism which are clinical competence, Good communication skills, sound understanding of ethics, accountability, altruism, excellence and humanism. We also aid in developing interpersonal skills and personality development of the future doctors.





MENTAL HEALTH UNIT


At most priority of Doctors must be self sanity , though the tiring schedules and collosal syllabus of med school takes a toll on our mental health, medicos are required to be mentally stable and sound since they deal with lives. NM offers listening ears and leaning shoulders for those who need mental support and this service is exhibited by professionals.





RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMEN UNIT


Namma medicine open doors for the students to explore the field of research which adds weight to their experience and knowledge and encourage the future doctors to focus on discovering and innovating health aids. NM provides the opportunity to be part of research works conducted by authorized organizations





COMMUNITY SERVICE UNIT


As medicos have surrendered to serve, Namma medicine takes a step forward by meeting the basic needs of the community. So as to make the future healers realize the pain and suffering of the community they are surrounded by and be more efficient in providing the health care to the needful.
